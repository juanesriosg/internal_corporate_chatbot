# Benefits FAQ

## Health Benefits

Full-time employees may enroll in medical, dental, and vision plans during onboarding or the annual enrollment window. Coverage starts on the first day of the month after the employee start date.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers are eligible for sixteen weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers are eligible for six weeks of paid parental leave. Leave must be coordinated with the employee's manager and HR business partner.

## Learning Budget

Full-time employees receive an annual learning budget of USD 1,200 for courses, books, certifications, and conferences related to their role. Manager approval is required before purchase.

## Wellness Benefit

Employees may expense up to USD 50 per month for wellness activities such as gym memberships, meditation apps, or fitness classes.
