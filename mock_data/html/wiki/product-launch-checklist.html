Readiness Gates

  • Product brief approved by product lead.
  • Security review completed for data or auth changes.
  • Support enablement article drafted.
  • Analytics events reviewed by data team.
  • Rollback plan documented for engineering changes.

Launch Communications

Marketing owns external messaging. Product owns internal launch notes. Support owns customer-facing help-center updates.

Post-launch Review

Within ten business days of launch, the product owner should review adoption, support tickets, reliability metrics, and user feedback.
